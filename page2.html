Here are some new hobbies to try

Baking

You have plenty of time on your hands! This is the perfect time to try making bread or that dish you always wanted to make but never found the time to.

Read a Book

Find that book on your shelf you've forgotten about. Check out what your local library offers for ebooks and audio recordings. Make your own Zoom book club with your friends and family.

Plant Something

Order a growing kit off of Amazon and see if you have a green thumb.

Try a Puzzle

Set it up on a table and work on it whenever you need a break.

Sew a reusable mask

Find a pattern online and use an old t-shirt. If you don't have a machine then try doing it by hand. Just watch your fingers.
